Privacy Policy for ReceiptVault.AI

Effective Date: October 2, 2024
Last Updated: October 2, 2024

1. Introduction

ReceiptVault.AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ReceiptVault.AI (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Any additional profile details you choose to provide

2.2 Receipt and Financial Data

  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts you capture using the camera
  • Extracted Data: Store names, purchase amounts, dates, categories, and warranty information extracted from receipts
  • Notes and Categories: Any manual entries, notes, or categorizations you add

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features used, crash reports
  • Camera Access: Camera permissions for receipt scanning (images processed locally)

2.4 Payment Information

  • Subscription Data: Subscription plan, payment status, billing history
  • Payment Processing: Payment information is processed securely through Stripe and Google Play Billing

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Store and organize your receipt data
  • Perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) on receipt images
  • Provide warranty tracking and reminders
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Generate expense reports and analytics

3.2 Account Management

  • Create and maintain your user account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Manage subscription services
  • Provide customer support

3.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features and services

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Data is securely stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • Image Storage: Receipt images are stored in secure cloud storage with encryption
  • Local Storage: Some data may be cached locally on your device for offline access

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and user authentication

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT sell your personal data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our app:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions
  • Google Play: App distribution and in-app purchases
  • OCR Services: Text extraction from receipt images

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Update: Modify your account information and receipt data
  • Delete: Remove individual receipts or your entire account
  • Export: Download your data in PDF or Excel format (Premium feature)

6.2 Data Portability

You can export your receipt data at any time through the app's export feature.

6.3 Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us or using the in-app deletion feature.

7. Children's Privacy

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our app may use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and session management

  • Crash reporting and debugging

10. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services:

  • Camera API: For receipt scanning
  • Cloud Storage: For data backup and sync
  • Payment Processors: For subscription management
  • Analytics: For app improvement (anonymized data only)

11. Data Retention

  • Receipt Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account

  • Deleted Data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request
